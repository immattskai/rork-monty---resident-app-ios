# Hide Board tile from non-board residents

**Change**

- The Board entry point on the home dashboard will only appear for residents who are active board members.
- Non-board residents won't see the Board tile at all, and the home grid will reflow so there's no empty space where it used to sit.
- Since the tab is now fully hidden, the "Access Restricted" cards inside the four Board sub-tabs are no longer reachable and will be removed to keep things tidy.

**Result**

- Regular residents see a cleaner dashboard without a tile they can't use.
- Board members continue to see the Board tile and everything inside it works exactly as before.
