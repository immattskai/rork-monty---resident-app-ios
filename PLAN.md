# Stop the top of Ask Monty from fading into a shadow

**The problem**

The Home screen has a soft fade just below the building photo that's meant to dissolve cards as they scroll up. Right now the Ask Monty card sits slightly too high — its top edge (the "Ask Monty" title) overlaps that fade, so it looks dimmed/shadowed at the top.

**The fix**

- Remove the dissolve! 

No other layout or content changes.
